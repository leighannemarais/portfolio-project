About me

Leigh-Anne Marais

Modeling has always been a passion of mine from stealing my older sisters makeup at the age of 8 to always wanting to be the most fashionable one in the room. My mom would always comment how photogenic I am and I only believed it once I seriously got behind a camera.
I wanted to pursue my dream to which I joined a modeling agency where I learnt commercial and runway modeling, and that's when I realized how fun modeling can be and that if you enjoy what you do - you'll never work a day in your life. Working with big brands would be an achievement but it is not a priority to me. As long as I am behind a camera, I am happy!
I am a goal getter and I work hard for things that I want. I also work well in competitive environments because who doesn't want to be the best?
One of the beautiful things about modeling is the different parts of the world that you get to see as well as the different cultures you get to experience. I love meeting new people and I look forward to what modeling has in store for me.
